Policies: Know Before You Go

To ensure a seamless and enjoyable stay at The Gatsby Grande, we encourage all our guests to familiarize themselves with our policies regarding cancellation, check-in/check-out times, and accepted payment methods.

Cancellation Policy: We understand that travel plans can change unexpectedly. To provide peace of mind, we offer flexible cancellation options. Cancellations made up to 48 hours before your scheduled arrival will receive a full refund. For cancellations made within the 48-hour window, a one-night charge may apply. We highly recommend reviewing specific rates at the time of booking, as some exclusive offers may have different terms.

Check-In and Check-Out Times: Our standard check-in time is 3 PM, allowing us sufficient time to prepare your room with the care and mindfulness you deserve. If you arrive earlier, we encourage you to enjoy our lounge and take advantage of our complimentary welcome beverages while you await your room. Our check-out time is at 11 AM; however, we offer flexible check-out options upon request to accommodate your travel needs, subject to availability.

Accepted Payment Methods: To ensure a hassle-free payment experience, we accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and mobile payment options. For a smooth check-in process, please ensure that the payment method used for booking matches the name of the person checking in. Additionally, a valid photo ID will be required at check-in.

By reviewing these policies before your stay, you can focus on enjoying the luxurious experience we offer at The Gatsby Grande, from our elegant accommodations to the exceptional service provided by our staff. Should you have any questions or need assistance, please do not hesitate to contact our front desk team, who will be more than happy to assist you.
