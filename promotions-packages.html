Promotions and Packages: Indulge in Exclusive Deals

At the Gatsby Grande, we believe that every stay should be a unique experience brimming with luxury and comfort. That is why we offer a variety of enticing promotions and carefully curated packages tailored to suit the diverse needs of our guests. From romantic getaways to family fun, explore our exclusive offers designed to enhance your stay.

Romantic Getaway Package:

Ignite the spark of romance with our special Romantic Getaway Package, perfect for couples looking to escape the ordinary. For $350 per night, this package includes a beautifully appointed room with stunning views, a bottle of champagne upon arrival, and a candlelit dinner for two at our acclaimed restaurant. Additionally, enjoy a complimentary late checkout and a special gift from our curated selection of artisan chocolates. This package invites you to unwind and reconnect amidst the elegance of The Gatsby Grande.

Family Fun Package:

Transform your family vacation into an adventure with our Family Fun Package! For $400 per night, you will enjoy spacious accommodations that can comfortably host up to four guests. This package includes breakfast for the entire family, a day pass to local attractions, and complimentary tickets to our popular weekend movie nights in our garden courtyard. Arts and Culture Package:

Immerse yourself in the local arts scene with our Arts and Culture Package, designed for art lovers and culture enthusiasts. Priced at $450 for a two-night stay, this package offers tickets to a local art exhibit, a guided historical tour of the area, and a complimentary art-inspired cocktail at our rooftop bar. Guests will also receive a bespoke art guide, highlighting galleries, studios, and cultural hotspots nearby.

Weekend Escape Special:

Take advantage of our Weekend Escape Special available for $299 per night! This promotion includes a complimentary breakfast buffet for two and access to our spa facilities, where you can unwind with a voucher for a signature massage. This package allows you to enjoy a rejuvenating weekend filled with comfort and style at The Gatsby Grande.

Group Retreat Package:

Planning a corporate getaway or a special reunion? Our Group Retreat Package caters to groups of ten or more and starts at $250 per night per guest. This all-inclusive package features meeting room access, breakfast and lunch, and a cocktail reception. Tailored team-building activities and guided local tours can be added for an additional fee, ensuring an unforgettable experience for all.
